About

In today's world of constant information production and consumption , it is a fairly a regular occurance for someone to hear a song on a station and miss out remnbering its name. Our music station database navigation tool seeks to solve this problem by better connecting station owners and station listeners. Our tool seeks to provide music station owners with the ability to easily list the songs they are currently hosting. Our tool also seeks to provide station listeners with the ability to locate songs in a given station.
